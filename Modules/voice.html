AI Voice Agent for Restaurants

An AI-powered voice assistant that acts as a drive-thru receptionist. Automates the full order-taking flow using speech recognition, NLP, and AI logic — handling calls, orders, and menu queries end-to-end.

Key Features
🎙️
Speech Recognition
Converts customer speech to text in real time, supporting natural conversational patterns including interruptions and corrections.
🧠
NLP and Intent Detection
Understands ordering, menu queries, order modifications, and pricing requests — routing each to the correct response flow.
🍔
Order Management
Takes and confirms orders, handles customizations, suggests popular items or add-ons, and calculates totals.
📞
Call Automation
Handles inbound restaurant calls end-to-end without human involvement, reducing staff workload during peak hours.
Tech Stack
Python Speech Recognition NLP LangChain AI Logic
