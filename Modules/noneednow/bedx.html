HIS Module · Real-Time Bed Management

Real-Time Bed Dashboard

Interactive visual floor map representing each hospital department floor-by-floor, divided into rooms with a physical representation of bed distribution. Designed to give hospital staff full real-time visibility and control over every bed.

Key Features
🗺️
Floor-by-Floor Map
Each department is rendered as a visual floor showing rooms and their exact bed layout, mirroring the physical hospital structure.
🎨
Color-Coded Status
Every bed is color-coded by status: occupied, clean, reserved, broken, or booked — giving instant visual clarity at a glance.
🖱️
Click to Change Status
Click any bed to open the status menu and update it instantly across the map.
👤
Patient Info on Hover
Hovering over any occupied bed shows the patient's name, admission reason, and current status in a tooltip.
